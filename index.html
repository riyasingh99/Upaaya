Our Mission

Empowering Individuals to Take Control of Their Health

At Upayaa, our mission is to revolutionize the healthcare industry by harnessing the power of technology. At the heart of our mission is the belief that individuals should have the tools and knowledge to take control of their own health. We aim to empower people with accessible and user-friendly health tech solutions that enable them to make informed decisions, monitor their well-being, and actively participate in their healthcare journey.







Better Brain, Mental health and Behavioural health through technology and training

"Reimagining Mental Wellness with Technology"

At Upayaa, we are passionate about enhancing brain health, mental well-being, and behavioral health through the power of technology and training. We believe that every individual deserves access to effective solutions that promote optimal brain function and foster positive mental health.

By leveraging the latest advancements in neuroscience and behavioral science, we aim to revolutionize the way we understand, diagnose, and treat brain-related conditions. Together, let's embark on a journey towards better brain health and a happier, more fulfilling life.

Our Story...

Achieving good physical and mental health is Multi-sectoral and involves social determinants of health like poverty , housing , finance along with a clinical definition. Mental or physical health does not only lie in the hands of a doctor or a hospital but also in the systems where a person lives or works. Example a good work culture prevents disease/hazards by inculcating good physical and mental habits , safety measures. Every business or system will be a embedded with a health well being function or digital push as a result of the pandemic.

UpayaaHealth starts from a personal itch of seeing family members going through chronic diseases physical and mental, falling through the cracks in the healthcare system and finding no sustainable solutions which could support their condition which lasted their lifetimes. It led to despair, anguish , decrease in quality of life and financial problems.
